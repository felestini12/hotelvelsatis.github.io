The Velsatis Hotel is located in the heart of the center of Beni Mellal in an extremely characteristic, quiet and lively area, within walking distance of all the sites. It is surrounded by the extraordinary beauty of the buildings, stores ,countryside. Upon entering this charming hotel in Beni Mellal, you will immediately feel its special intimate atmosphere that will give you the impression of being in your own home. Every detail has been chosen with passion and each room is worth a visit. The style of the Velsatis hotel mixes valuable antiques with an unexpected eclectic contemporary touch. The special charm and warm atmosphere of Velsatis will make you feel like a true Mellali in Beni Mellal..
